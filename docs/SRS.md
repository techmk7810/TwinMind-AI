# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) defines the functional and non-functional requirements for TwinMind AI, an enterprise decision intelligence platform designed to assist retail businesses in making data-driven decisions through Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twin technology, predictive analytics, scenario simulation, and explainable recommendations.

This document serves as the primary reference for software design, development, testing, deployment, maintenance, and future enhancements.

---

## 1.2 Scope

TwinMind AI provides an integrated platform that enables organizations to:

* Upload business datasets.
* Validate and clean business data.
* Build a Digital Twin of business operations.
* Forecast future business performance.
* Simulate alternative business strategies.
* Receive AI-generated recommendations.
* Generate executive reports.
* Monitor overall business health.

The first release targets medium-sized retail organizations operating approximately 10–100 stores.

---

## 1.3 Objectives

The primary objectives of TwinMind AI are:

* Improve business decision quality.
* Reduce uncertainty through predictive analytics.
* Enable scenario-based business planning.
* Increase operational efficiency.
* Deliver explainable AI recommendations.
* Support strategic decision-making using enterprise AI technologies.

## 1.5 Definitions

| Term                  | Description                                                                                 |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Digital Twin          | Virtual representation of business operations.                                              |
| Predictive Analytics  | Machine learning models used to estimate future outcomes.                                   |
| Multi-Agent AI        | Multiple specialized AI agents collaborating to solve business problems.                    |
| Explainable AI (XAI)  | AI techniques that provide understandable explanations for predictions and recommendations. |
| Business Health Score | Composite score representing overall organizational performance.                            |
| Workspace             | An isolated business environment containing users, datasets, reports, and AI models.        |

# 2. Overall Description

## 2.1 Product Perspective

TwinMind AI is a web-based enterprise platform consisting of multiple interconnected services.

Major platform components include:

* Frontend Web Application
* Backend API
* Authentication Service
* AI Services
* Digital Twin Engine
* Simulation Engine
* Forecasting Engine
* Reporting Module
* Database
* File Storage

Each module communicates through secure APIs and is designed for future scalability.

---

## 2.2 Product Goals

TwinMind AI aims to:

* Transform raw business data into actionable intelligence.
* Replace manual spreadsheet-based decision-making.
* Enable proactive business planning.
* Improve operational transparency.
* Support executive-level decision-making through AI.

---

## 2.3 Business Value

The platform provides value by:

* Reducing decision uncertainty.
* Improving forecasting capabilities.
* Detecting operational risks.
* Identifying optimization opportunities.
* Supporting strategic planning.
